Where we open in 2027

The flagship in Tekstilshchiki — within walking distance of Yardyam mosque and the Cathedral Mosque, at the heart of Moscow's Muslim community. Plan B: Altufyevo (Cathedral Mosque area) — backup location in case of negotiation or grid hold-up. A pipeline of 3–4 alternative sites with ready 80 kW capacity is in motion. Y1 = 2027 with 11–16 month grid lead time.

Location risk mitigation

Time to flagship opening — 9–15 months after Series A closing (accounting for 80 kW grid lead time 11–16 months). A pipeline of 3–4 alternative sites with ready capacity is in motion as backup. Plan B Altufyevo is formalised as a backup scenario. An LOI with the landlord is signed before Series A closing — providing investor comfort and signalling rigour.

Further scaling (Y2–Y5)

Y2 · 2028: launch of central production kitchen, Moscow density expansion (+10 compact), Saint Petersburg flagship prep.

Y3 · 2029: Kazan flagship, start of compact franchising. Regions: Ufa, Surgut, Makhachkala, Novy Urengoy.

Y5 · 2031: 65 stores across 6+ cities. 3 flagships + 62 compact stores. Premium niche leader of Russian halal retail for practicing Muslims.
