What we do

Three pillars of Chapan

Not a "halal aisle" inside a mainstream chain. Not a corner shop by the mosque. A supermarket chain with a single standard, a visible halal committee and Uzbek hot food on the counter.

Standard

100% halal on the shelf

The full assortment is certified. Dual certification: SMR + Tatarstan DUM. QR traceability from farm to shelf.

In-store deli and bakery

Tandoor flatbread, plov, samsa, manty — made on site. The smell of fresh bread and a hot food counter as a magnet for families.
